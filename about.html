### About Me Hey there! I'm Clarissa, a senior in Leverett studying Linguistics with a bit of Computer Science. This is my website where I document my work for PHYSCI 70: Intro to Digital Fabrication. I took GENED 1080 in the fall of 2021 and had a great time learning about 3D printing, laser cutting, and basic electronics. For my final project in that course, I made this lyre: ![the front of a small wooden lyre](./harp-front.jpeg) ![the back of the same lyre](./harp-back.jpeg) (It's about 2 feet tall, for reference.) I also spend a lot of time outside of class working backstage on theatrical shows. Specifically, I design, program, and operate the lighting. Through stage lighting, I've learned about 2D drafting with CAD software and gotten pretty good at ETC console programming. ![photo of a dancer jumping on a stage, lit with various colors of light.] (./hbc.jpg) This is a photo of a ballet show I lit in the spring of 2022.
